Vocabee Sprouts

Senior KG ( Age 5–6 Years )

The Milestone
Year

Transitioning from “Learning to Read” to "Reading to Learn."

Learning Journey:

Phonics & Decoding

  • The Foundation Refresh: Revision of 26 alphabet phonemes and CVC words (Short Vowels).
  • Digraphs: Introduction to "letter teams" - ch, sh, wh, th, ck, ng.
  • Consonant Blends: Beginning - bl, fl, br, gr (and more), Ending - nk, sk, mp, nd (and more).
  • Long Vowels: In depth.
  • The "Bossy R": Mastery of r-controlled vowels.

Literacy Foundations

  • Sentence Architecture: Moving from single words to structured sentence building.
  • Sight Words: High-frequency "heart words" to build reading stamina.
  • Reading to Learn: Using short non-fiction snippets (e.g., "All About Ants") where kids extract information after reading and answer "why" and "how."

The Expression Studio

  • Vocabulary Play: Multi-sensory learning through purposeful songs, actions, and games.
  • Oral Narrative: Weekly story-time sessions to build comprehension and public speaking.
  • Active Listening: Training the ear to distinguish complex sounds and follow multi-step instructions.
  • Creativity Pillar: Making stories to boost imagination and foster emotional intelligence.
